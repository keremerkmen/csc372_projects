Discover the URI Sailing Team

The URI Sailing Team is a dedicated and competitive group of student-athletes representing the University of Rhode Island in the New England Intercollegiate Sailing Association (NEISA). Competing in over two dozen regattas each spring season, our team travels across the Northeast and beyond, participating in conference championships, regional qualifiers, and national-level events.

In Spring 2025, we raced in 29 regattas, including the prestigious NEISA Women's Fleet Race Championship, the George Morris Trophy, and the 87th Boston Dinghy Club Challenge Cup. Our schedule reflects our commitment to excellence, discipline, and team spirit both on and off the water.

Whether you're an experienced sailor or new to the sport, the URI Sailing Team offers opportunities to grow, compete, and build lifelong friendships. Join us in pursuing excellence through teamwork, resilience, and a love for sailing.

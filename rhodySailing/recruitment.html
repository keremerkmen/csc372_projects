Join the URI Sailing Team

Are you passionate about sailing and ready to represent URI in one of the most competitive college sailing conferences in the country? The URI Sailing Team competes in over 25 regattas each spring season across the NEISA region, including events like the George Morris Trophy, Southern 7, and the NEISA Championships.

We welcome all levels of experience, from experienced high school sailors looking to continue their racing careers, to newcomers eager to learn. Our recruitment process includes introductory meetings, tryouts, and mentorship from returning varsity sailors. We train regularly on Narragansett Bay, focusing on boat handling, tactics, and teamwork.

Interested in joining? Reach out to our recruitment coordinator at recruit@sailing.uri.edu for information on tryout dates, team expectations, and how to get involved.
